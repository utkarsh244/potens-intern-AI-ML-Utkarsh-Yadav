Leave and Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Purpose
This policy outlines the paid and unpaid leave entitlements available to full-time employees.

Annual PTO Allowance
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month. PTO can be used for vacation, personal time, or illness. Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over into the following calendar year; any additional unused balance is forfeited.

Sick Leave
In addition to PTO, employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year for their own illness or to care for an immediate family member. Sick leave does not carry over and does not need to be requested in advance.

Parental Leave
Employees who have completed at least six months of service are eligible for 16 weeks of paid parental leave following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. Parental leave may be taken continuously or split into two blocks within the first 12 months.

Bereavement Leave
Employees may take up to 5 paid days of bereavement leave for the death of an immediate family member, and up to 2 paid days for the death of a close relative outside the immediate family.

Requesting Leave
PTO requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned absences. Sick leave and bereavement leave may be reported after the fact, as soon as reasonably possible.
