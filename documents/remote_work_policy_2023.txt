Remote Work Policy (Effective January 2023)

Purpose
This policy defines the rules for remote and hybrid work arrangements at the company. It applies to all full-time employees across all departments.

Office Attendance Requirement
All employees are required to work from the office a minimum of three (3) days per week. The designated in-office days are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Employees may work remotely on Monday and Friday at their manager's discretion. Fully remote arrangements are not permitted under this policy except for employees with an approved medical or disability accommodation on file with Human Resources.

Requesting Exceptions
Employees who wish to deviate from the three-day office requirement must submit a written request to their manager and to HR at least two weeks in advance. Exceptions are granted on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

Equipment and Expenses
Employees who are approved for remote work days will be provided a laptop and a one-time home office stipend of $300. Ongoing internet costs are the responsibility of the employee.

Performance Expectations
Managers are expected to monitor attendance compliance and may escalate repeated non-compliance to HR. Failure to meet the in-office attendance requirement without an approved exception may be noted in an employee's performance review.

Review
This policy will be reviewed annually by the People Operations team and may be updated to reflect changing business needs.
