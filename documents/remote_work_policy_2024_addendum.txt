Hybrid and Remote Work Addendum (Effective March 2024)

Purpose
This addendum updates the company's approach to remote and hybrid work in light of employee feedback and market conditions. Where this addendum conflicts with earlier policy documents, this addendum governs.

Fully Remote Work Permitted
Effective immediately, employees may choose to work fully remotely on an indefinite basis, subject to manager approval of their initial request. There is no longer a mandated minimum number of in-office days for employees who select the fully remote option. Employees who prefer a hybrid schedule may continue to coordinate in-office days directly with their team.

Requesting Fully Remote Status
Employees who wish to move to fully remote status should notify their manager in writing. No HR pre-approval or medical documentation is required for standard fully remote arrangements.

Equipment and Expenses
Employees approved for fully remote work will receive a laptop, a monitor, and a one-time home office stipend of $500. The company will also reimburse up to $40 per month toward home internet costs.

Performance Expectations
Attendance compliance monitoring tied to a fixed number of in-office days is discontinued for fully remote employees. Managers should instead evaluate employees based on output and deliverables.

Review
This addendum supersedes conflicting sections of the January 2023 Remote Work Policy and will be reviewed by People Operations after twelve months.
